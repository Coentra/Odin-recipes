Homemade Frenchfries

Description

Yummy, easy and healthy homemade Frenchfries

Ingredients

  • 4 big potatoes
  • dill spice
  • Cheese
  • Paprika powder
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes
  2. Cut them up into thin slices.
  3. Add the slices into a bowl and add the seasoning/li>
  4. Put them into the earifryer and fry for 20 minutes on 200 degree celcius
  5. Add dill and serve
