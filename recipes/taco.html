Taco

Description

Taco is a very traditional cusine in Norway. We usually say that Friday is Taco Friday. The good thing about taco is that you can enjo Taco in a lot of ways. Some likes to have sour cream, some like pineapple. The possibilities is endless.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lettuce
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 Paprika
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 Ground beef
  • Spice mix
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • 4 Tortialls

Steps

  1. Cut up all the vedgetables and either but them all in a big bowl or have them each in separate bowls
  2. Cut up the ground beef and coock it in a pan. When the meat is done, mix in the spice mix and add 1 dl of water. Blend together and blend in and let it coock for another 2 minutes.
  3. Heat up the Tortillas in the microwave
  4. Put everything on the table and serve
